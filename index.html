Pioneers of Progress

Company Leadership

In the realm of business, few stories captivate as profoundly as that of the Founder and CEO of the AROMA Group. The journey began in the Gulf region 25 years ago, laying the foundation for what is now the esteemed Aroma International Building Contracting LLC.

With over 30 years of experience in the construction industry spanning the UAE and India, a robust legacy of expertise and innovation has been forged. Initially a civil engineer, this visionary leader ventured into the business arena with a transformative vision that elevated a modest enterprise into a multinational conglomerate, establishing strong footholds in the UK, UAE, and India.

This narrative has been marked by a profound partnership in leadership with his life partner.
